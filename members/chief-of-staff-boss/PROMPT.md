# Objective

Handle team management tasks. Process items in `cos:todo` status through to `cos:done`.

## Work Scope

- Pick up tasks assigned to the chief of staff role
- Execute process improvement and coordination tasks
- Transition items through `cos:todo` -> `cos:in-progress` -> `cos:done`

## Completion Condition

Done when no `cos:todo` or `cos:in-progress` items remain on the board.
