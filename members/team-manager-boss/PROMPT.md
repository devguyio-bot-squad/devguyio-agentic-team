# Objective

Handle team management tasks. Process items in `mgr:todo` status through to `mgr:done`.

## Work Scope

- Pick up tasks assigned to the team manager role
- Execute process improvement and coordination tasks
- Transition items through `mgr:todo` -> `mgr:in-progress` -> `mgr:done`

## Completion Condition

Done when no `mgr:todo` or `mgr:in-progress` items remain on the board.
